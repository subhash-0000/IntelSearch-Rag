# Enterprise SLA Commitments

For enterprise customers, monthly uptime objective is 99.9%.
Service credits begin when monthly uptime falls below 99.5%.
Priority support response targets:
- P1 critical issues: first response within 15 minutes.
- P2 high issues: first response within 1 hour.
- P3 normal issues: first response within 8 business hours.
Maintenance windows are announced at least 72 hours in advance.
