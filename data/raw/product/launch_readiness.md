# Product Launch Readiness Checklist

## Two Weeks Before Launch
- Freeze high-risk changes.
- Confirm rollback plan and ownership.
- Validate alerts and dashboards.

## Three Days Before Launch
- Conduct go/no-go review with Engineering, Product, Support, and Security.
- Verify support runbook and known issue list.

## Launch Day
- Monitor error rates, latency, and conversion metrics hourly.
- Keep incident response channel staffed for 24 hours.
