# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility
Remote work is allowed for roles with no on-site operational dependency.

## Core Collaboration Hours
Employees must be available from 10:00 to 16:00 in their home timezone.

## Security Requirements
- Use company-managed devices.
- Use VPN for internal systems.
- Avoid public Wi-Fi without mobile hotspot fallback.

## Travel and Tax Considerations
Working from a different country for more than 30 days requires HR and Legal approval.
