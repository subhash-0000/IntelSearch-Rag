Paid Leave Policy

Employees accrue 1.75 days of paid leave per month of active employment.
Unused leave can be carried over up to a cap of 15 days.
Manager approval is required for leave longer than 5 consecutive business days.
For unplanned sick leave, employees should inform their manager before the start of their shift whenever possible.
For medical leave exceeding 3 days, documentation may be requested by HR.
