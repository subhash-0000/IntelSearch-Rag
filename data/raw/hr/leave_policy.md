# Employee Leave Policy

## Annual Leave
- Full-time employees receive 20 paid annual leave days.
- Leave requests should be submitted at least 5 business days in advance.

## Sick Leave
- Sick leave does not require prior approval for up to 2 consecutive days.
- For longer absence, medical documentation may be requested.

## Caregiver Leave
Employees may use up to 5 paid caregiver leave days per year for direct family care responsibilities.
